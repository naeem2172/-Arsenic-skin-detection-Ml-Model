Skin care for arsenic-affected individuals: Keep skin moisturized to prevent cracking, especially on palms and soles. Use sunscreen (SPF 30 or higher) to protect affected areas from sun damage. Avoid harsh soaps and excessive washing. Wear protective gloves when doing manual work to prevent further damage. Gently exfoliate keratotic areas as advised by a dermatologist. Do not attempt to remove lesions at home. Monitor any changes in existing lesions or new growths and report to your doctor. Maintain good hygiene with safe water. Seek medical attention for any signs of infection (redness, swelling, discharge) in skin lesions.
