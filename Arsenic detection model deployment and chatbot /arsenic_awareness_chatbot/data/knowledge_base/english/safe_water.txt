To prevent arsenic exposure from water: Test your water source at least annually, especially if using well or groundwater. Use water filters certified to remove arsenic (look for NSF/ANSI 58 or 53 certification for arsenic reduction). Consider switching to piped water from treated municipal supplies if available. Rainwater harvesting (with proper treatment) can be an alternative in some areas. Avoid using untested well water for drinking or cooking. Community-level solutions include deep tube wells that tap into arsenic-free aquifers and centralized water treatment systems. Educate family members about the importance of using only safe water sources.
